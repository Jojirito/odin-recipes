Siu Mai - Chinese Steamed Dumplings

Description

Make your Dim Sum favorite at home with this quick and easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pork shoulder butt
  • 4 oz shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 4 pieces dried shiitake mushroom
  • 20 pieces siu mai wrappers
  • 0.50 oz carrot (minced for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 0.50 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 0.50 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 0.50 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Chop and prepare ingredients
  2. Make marinade
  3. Marinate ingredients
  4. Fold dumplings
  5. Steam dumplings
  6. Enjoy!
